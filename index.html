Our Focus: Colombo Stock Exchange

Investera (Private) Limited is a Sri Lankan-based company focused on CSE listed stocks with Sharia-compliant strategies.

The Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) is Sri Lanka's principal stock exchange with over 250 listed companies across diverse industries. It provides a regulated, transparent, and liquid marketplace — and Investera leverages this ecosystem to build long-term value.
