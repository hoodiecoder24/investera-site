Understanding the Colombo Stock Exchange

Market Overview

The Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) is Sri Lanka's principal stock exchange, established in 1985. It serves as the primary marketplace for equity securities in Sri Lanka.

  • 250+ Listed Companies across diverse sectors
  • ASPI Index - All Share Price Index
  • S&P SL20 Index - Top 20 companies
  • Regulated Market by SEC Sri Lanka
