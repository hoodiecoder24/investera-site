About Investera

Sharia-Compliant Ethical Investing in Sri Lanka

Company Overview

Investera (Private) Limited is a Sri Lankan-based company focused on CSE listed stocks with Sharia-compliant strategies.

Our Foundation

Established as a Private Limited Company in Sri Lanka, Investera is dedicated to providing ethical investment solutions that align with Islamic finance principles while maximizing returns for our investors.

Market Focus

We specialize in Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) listed equities, leveraging our deep understanding of the Sri Lankan market to identify high-potential investment opportunities.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a dynamic and high-performing equity investment company in Sri Lanka, driven by data, ethics, and long-term value creation. We aim to be the leading Sharia-compliant investment firm in the region.

Our Mission

Build a diversified portfolio and outperform market benchmarks while maximizing shareholder value through Sharia-compliant strategies. We are committed to ethical investing that creates sustainable wealth.

Sharia & Ethical Principles

Our Ethical Framework
  • No investments in interest-based (riba) activities
  • Exclusion of gambling and speculative activities
  • Prohibition of investments in alcohol and tobacco
  • No involvement in pork-related businesses
  • Transparent and ethical business practices
Investment Criteria
  • Thorough Sharia compliance screening
  • Focus on real economic activities
  • Asset-backed investments only
  • Regular compliance monitoring
  • Ethical governance standards
